How To Use This Toolkit

×

Step 1: Understand the Risk Matrix

The risk matrix provides a visual framework to assess the likelihood and impact of various risks. Risks are categorized as Low, Medium, High, or Critical based on their combined score.

Step 2: Complete the Self-Assessment

Score each risk factor based on likelihood (how often it might occur) and impact (the severity if it does occur). Use the 1-4 scale provided.

Step 3: Review Your Dashboard

After completing the assessment, review your risk summary dashboard. This provides an overall picture of your risk profile.

Step 4: Take Recommended Actions

Follow the recommended actions for each risk band. Higher risk scores require more immediate and thorough mitigation strategies.
